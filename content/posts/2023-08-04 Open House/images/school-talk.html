We will continue to work building towards our Abingdon vision.  It states "Abingdon builds meaningful relationships by fostering an inclusive environment throughout our community.  We are committed to challenging and empowering all learners."  All students, staff and community members at Abingdon should see themselves in this vision.

Our Open House will be held on Thursday, August 24. Please come at this time to meet your child's teacher, drop off supplies, and learn about Abingdon.

  • Last names A-M are scheduled to come from 1:30-2:15
  • Last names N-Z are scheduled to come from 2:15-3:00

The first day of school is Monday, August 28 for Kindergarten through 5th Grade.  The first day of school for PreKindergarten is Tuesday, August 29.

School hours are 7:50-2:40, with doors opening at 7:30.

Parents of Preschoolers and Kindergarteners are welcome to come into the building on the first day of school to drop off their child at the doorway to the classroom.  We ask parents to leave the building no later than 8:10 on the first day so that our school year can begin.  

Back to School night is on Wednesday, September 6 from 6:30-7:45.  This event is only for parents, so please make arrangements for your children so you can join us that evening.

We look forward to having you with us!
